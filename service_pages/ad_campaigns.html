We Only Profit If You Do

We operate off of a Cost Per Lead (CPL) Business Model. A "lead" is a potential customer who has shown interest in your product/service by taking a specific action: filling a form, clicking an ad, visiting a website, etc. This means we only get paid if we deliver you results.

What Your Investment Covers

  • Ad Spend: The budget allocated directly to paying Meta (Facebook and Instagram) for running your ads.
    $500 – $5,000/month depending on target market and competition.
  • Ad Creation & Management: The costs associated with designing, testing, and optimizing ads using premium digital tools and based on statistical analysis.
    $300 – $2,000/month depending on campaign complexity and creative needs.
  • Lead Compensation: A specified amount for each lead we generate you. If we fail to generate your business leads we make nothing.
    $10 – $800 per lead depending on industry and customer value.

How We Price A Lead

Our pricing is tailored to your specific needs and is influenced by several key factors:

  • Your Industry: Different industries have varying levels of competition and ad costs.
    Example Ranges: Real Estate ($30–$150), Legal ($150–$500), Financial Services ($50–$350).
  • Lead Quality: We focus on delivering high-quality leads that are more likely to convert. The better the lead, the higher the cost.
    Higher intent leads (e.g., booked calls) may cost more but yield higher ROI.
  • Average Customer Value (ACV): The most important factor. Customer value is determined by how much average revenue a lead generates your business. A higher customer value justifies a larger investment in lead acquisition.
    CPL typically ranges from 5%–25% of your ACV. For example, a $2,000 ACV might justify a $100–$400 lead price.
